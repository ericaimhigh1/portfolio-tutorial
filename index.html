About

Hello there, I am Eric, a Frontend Web Develper and a UI/UX designer. I am from Odumase - Ghana, but currently based in Lilongwe, Malawi. My life? I simply wake up every morning, grab a cup rice porridge and start to make love with the keyboard, and the mouse. Music is always a catalyst in this love making process.

People do say I should get a life, but what they dont know is that my life is different from theirs, at least the definitions.

I've had a youtube channel since the last four years, which has not seen any growth, but for months now, I have started uploading website development lessons. You should check out how great a teacher I am. It's called Eric Aimhigh
